Who is this luciously locked, ethnically ambiguous, scholar of excellence?

Jacob is a Virginia-born musician and software engineer, currently enrolled at Oberlin College. There, he studies computer science, mathematics, and jazz guitar.

On this page, you'll find all of his schemes. One of his computer programs is called MusicTranscriber, a program that will turn a single audio file into single-instrument-separated midi files and audio files. You can also find his plethora of original music.
